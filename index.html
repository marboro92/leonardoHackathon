What is it all about?

Collaboration and Fresh Ideas

Hackathon is an opportunity for everyone to work together to build and pitch fresh ideas to solve business challenges and improve products at Leonardo.

Anyone can join the hackathon, teams will need people to work on everything from product research and development to the final business pitch. You do not need to take on a role that resembles your current role at Leonardo, you can take this as a chance to expand your skill set!

What will I be working on?

Your own idea or your coworkers'. On the first day of hackathon you will sign up for one of the submitted ideas.

Anyone can submit an idea!

It just has to fit in with the theme for this hackathon.

The Theme: INTEGRATION

This hackathon will accept ideas that involve integrating our current system with other technologies to enhance performance or provide better customer experience.

Here are some examples to get you started:

  • Integrate native mobile cameras to upload directly into Vizlly or Vscape
  • Use chatops(bots) in HipChat or other communication forums to increase communication between teams and departments
  • Hit public APIs such as Google to help determine image duplication or stats

How do I participate?

RSVP and Start Brainstorming!

To participate simply accept the Hackathon Event in Outlook on August 31 and September 1st. 2 full days!

Have an Idea?

Send it in to hackathon@leonardo.com.

Be sure to check out the judging criteria when your idea is built (all categories are weighted evenly):

  1. Theme Relevance
  2. Business Potential (value to company, scalability etc.)
  3. Technical Difficulty
  4. Creativity (business pitch, different angle, implementation)
  5. Awesomeness
