Submit Your Ideas

You can submit ideas for the hackathon.

Ideas will be postyed on the first day of the hackathon and everyone will sign up for the idea they would like to work on.

THE THEME IS: INTEGRATION

This hackathon will accept ideas that involve integrating our current system with other technologies to enhance performance or provide better customer experience.

Here are some examples to get you started:

  • Integrate native mobile cameras to upload directly into vizlly or vscape
  • Use chatops(bots) in HipChat or other communication forums to increase communication between teams and departments
  • Hit public APIs such as Google to help determine image duplication or stats

The final product will be presented at the end of the hackathon given a score out of 20 for each of the following:

  • Theme Relevance
  • Business Potential (value to company, scalability etc.)
  • Technical Difficulty
  • Creativity (business pitch, different angle, implementation)
  • Awesomeness

Teams

On the first day of hackathon you will sign up for an idea you would like to work on. This will determine your team.

There are plenty of roles to be filled as part of a team. Take this opportunity to take on new challenges, you do not have to choose a role that resembles your current role at Leonardo.

Roles may vary depending on the idea but here are some roles that will probably need to be filled:

  • Team Leader: Can be anyone. In charge of ensuring everyone is on point, whether its delivering initial version of powerpoint presentation or adding feature/fixing a bug in the code.
  • Business/Marketing Value Team: in charge of creating the business pitch and marketing for the product, researching competitors, communicating the product's value
  • Coders/Testers
